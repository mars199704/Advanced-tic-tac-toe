Read the rules first

1. It's a harder tic-tac-toe

2. There are 9 boxes on the board and each of them has 9 cells

3. X goes first

4. If you place the mark on the right cell, the next player could only place the mark on the right box

5. Anyone who gets one line in the box win the game!!

6. If X wins the game at first, next time will be O goes first

7. Have fun !!!
